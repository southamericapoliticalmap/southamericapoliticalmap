    Map Of India Showing States

    Posted on March 7, 2023 by admin

    Introduction

    India is a vast country with 28 states and 8 union territories. The country is known for its diverse culture, traditions, and languages. The map of India shows the geographical boundaries of these states and union territories. In this article, we will discuss the map of India showing states, their location, and other important details.

    Location of States

    India is located in South Asia and shares borders with Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar. The country is divided into 28 states and 8 union territories, each with its own capital city. The states are located in different parts of the country and have their unique culture, traditions, and languages.

    North India

    North India consists of seven states, including Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, and Uttar Pradesh. The region is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and delicious food.

    East India

    East India comprises of eight states, including West Bengal, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Nagaland, and Tripura. The region is known for its tea plantations, diverse wildlife, and ancient temples.

    South India

    South India comprises of five states, including Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Telangana. The region is known for its beautiful beaches, lush green forests, and ancient temples.

    West India

    West India consists of four states, including Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Goa. The region is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture.

    Important Details

    The map of India shows the location of each state and union territory. It also provides information about the population, area, and official language of each state. The capital city of each state is also marked on the map.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, the map of India showing states is an important tool that provides information about the location, culture, and language of each state. It is useful for tourists, students, and anyone who wants to learn more about India. We hope this article has provided you with a comprehensive guide to the map of India showing states.

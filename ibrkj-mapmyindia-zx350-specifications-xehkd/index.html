    Mapmyindia Zx350 Specifications

    Posted on November 18, 2022 by admin

    A Brief Introduction to MapmyIndia

    MapmyIndia is an Indian technology company that specializes in digital maps, navigation, and location-based services. The company was founded in 1995 and has since then been providing innovative solutions to businesses and consumers alike. One of the latest products from MapmyIndia is the ZX350, a GPS navigator with advanced features and capabilities. In this article, we will take a closer look at the specifications of the MapmyIndia ZX350.

    Display and Design

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 features a 5-inch TFT LCD touch screen display with a resolution of 800×480 pixels. The device has a sleek and compact design, making it easy to carry around. The navigator comes with a windshield mount and a car charger, making it easy to use while driving.

    Navigation Features

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 is packed with advanced navigation features that make it easy to find your way around. The device comes preloaded with detailed maps of India, which include 10.63 million points of interest (POIs) and 2.2 million kilometers of road network. The navigator also features voice-guided turn-by-turn navigation, lane guidance, and speed limit alerts. The device also has a 3D landmark and building view, making it easy to identify your surroundings.

    Connectivity

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 comes with built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, making it easy to connect to the internet and other devices. The device also features a microSD card slot, which allows you to expand the storage capacity up to 32GB. The navigator also supports USB connectivity, which makes it easy to transfer data between the device and your computer.

    Camera and Multimedia

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 features a 5-megapixel rear camera, which can be used to capture images and videos. The device also has a built-in media player, which supports a wide range of audio and video formats. The navigator also features an FM radio, which allows you to listen to your favorite radio stations on the go.

    Battery Life

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 is powered by a 1500mAh rechargeable battery, which provides up to 3 hours of continuous use. The device also features a power-saving mode, which helps to conserve battery life when the navigator is not in use.

    Price and Availability

    The MapmyIndia ZX350 is priced at INR 9,990 and is available for purchase on the MapmyIndia website and other online retailers. The device comes with a 1-year warranty and free lifetime map updates.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, the MapmyIndia ZX350 is a feature-packed GPS navigator that offers advanced navigation features, connectivity options, and multimedia capabilities. The device is easy to use and comes with a sleek and compact design. If you are looking for a reliable and feature-packed GPS navigator, the MapmyIndia ZX350 is definitely worth considering.

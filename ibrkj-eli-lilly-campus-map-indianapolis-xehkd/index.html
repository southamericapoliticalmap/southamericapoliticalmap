    Eli Lilly Campus Map Indianapolis

    Posted on October 11, 2022 by admin

    Introduction

    Eli Lilly and Company is a pharmaceutical corporation that has been based in Indianapolis, Indiana since its founding in 1876. Today, the company has a sprawling campus in the heart of the city, with over 11,000 employees working on site. If you’re planning a visit to the Eli Lilly campus, it’s helpful to have a map on hand to navigate the various buildings and facilities.

    The Main Campus

    The main Eli Lilly campus is located in downtown Indianapolis, bounded by Delaware, McCarty, Virginia, and East Streets. The campus is divided into several sections, including the Lilly Corporate Center, the Technology Center, and the Manufacturing Center. The Corporate Center is home to the company’s administrative offices, while the Technology Center houses research and development facilities.

    Visitor Center

    If you’re visiting the Eli Lilly campus for the first time, stop by the Visitor Center to pick up a campus map and get information about tours and events. The Visitor Center is located on the first floor of the Corporate Center and is open Monday through Friday.

    Recreation Facilities

    In addition to its work facilities, the Eli Lilly campus also offers a variety of recreational amenities. The campus has a fitness center, basketball and volleyball courts, and a softball field. There are also walking paths and outdoor seating areas for employees and visitors to enjoy.

    Cafeterias and Dining Options

    When it’s time to grab a bite to eat, the Eli Lilly campus has several cafeterias and dining options. The Cafe Lilly is a popular spot for breakfast and lunch, offering a variety of hot and cold options. Other dining options include a Starbucks, Subway, and a convenience store.

    Parking and Transportation

    If you’re driving to the Eli Lilly campus, there are several parking garages available for employees and visitors. The company also offers a shuttle service to transport employees between buildings on the campus. If you’re taking public transportation, the campus is located near several bus stops and is a short walk from the downtown Indianapolis transit center.

    Green Initiatives

    Eli Lilly is committed to sustainability and has implemented several green initiatives on its campus. The company has a LEED-certified building, uses renewable energy sources, and has implemented recycling and composting programs.

    Community Involvement

    Eli Lilly is also involved in the Indianapolis community and supports several local organizations and initiatives. The company has a volunteer program that encourages employees to give back to the community through service projects and donations.

    Conclusion

    The Eli Lilly campus is a bustling hub of activity in downtown Indianapolis, with a variety of facilities and amenities for employees and visitors. Whether you’re exploring the campus for business or pleasure, be sure to grab a map and take advantage of all the campus has to offer.

    India Map With States And Their Capitals

    Posted on October 27, 2022 by admin

    Introduction

    India is a diverse country with 28 states and 8 union territories. Each state has its own unique culture, language, and traditions. It is important to know the map of India with states and their capitals to understand the geography of the country. In this article, we will discuss the India map with states and their capitals in 2023.

    The Northern States

    The northern region of India has seven states. Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Uttarakhand, Haryana, Delhi, and Uttar Pradesh. The capital of India, Delhi, is also located in this region. The iconic Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

    Jammu and Kashmir

    Jammu and Kashmir is known for its beautiful valleys, mountains, and lakes. The capital of Jammu and Kashmir is Srinagar in the summer and Jammu in the winter.

    Himachal Pradesh

    Himachal Pradesh is a famous tourist destination known for its scenic beauty and adventure sports. The capital of Himachal Pradesh is Shimla.

    Punjab

    Punjab is known for its rich culture and food. The capital of Punjab is Chandigarh, which is also the capital of Haryana.

    Uttarakhand

    Uttarakhand is known for its beautiful hill stations and pilgrimage sites. The capital of Uttarakhand is Dehradun.

    Haryana

    Haryana is known for its agricultural and industrial development. The capital of Haryana is Chandigarh.

    Delhi

    Delhi is the capital of India and is known for its rich history and culture. It is also the political and economic hub of the country.

    Uttar Pradesh

    Uttar Pradesh is the most populous state in India. It is known for its historical monuments and religious sites. The capital of Uttar Pradesh is Lucknow.

    The Eastern States

    The eastern region of India has eight states. Bihar, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Odisha, Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, and Nagaland.

    Bihar

    Bihar is known for its rich history and culture. The capital of Bihar is Patna.

    Jharkhand

    Jharkhand is known for its mineral resources and wildlife sanctuaries. The capital of Jharkhand is Ranchi.

    West Bengal

    West Bengal is known for its literature, music, and food. The capital of West Bengal is Kolkata.

    Odisha

    Odisha is known for its beautiful beaches and temples. The capital of Odisha is Bhubaneswar.

    Sikkim

    Sikkim is known for its scenic beauty and biodiversity. The capital of Sikkim is Gangtok.

    Assam

    Assam is known for its tea, silk, and wildlife. The capital of Assam is Dispur.

    Arunachal Pradesh

    Arunachal Pradesh is known for its diverse culture and natural beauty. The capital of Arunachal Pradesh is Itanagar.

    Nagaland

    Nagaland is known for its rich tribal culture and festivals. The capital of Nagaland is Kohima.

    The Western States

    The western region of India has six states. Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa, Dadra and Nagar Haveli, and Daman and Diu.

    Rajasthan

    Rajasthan is known for its forts, palaces, and desert. The capital of Rajasthan is Jaipur.

    Gujarat

    Gujarat is known for its art, culture, and industry. The capital of Gujarat is Gandhinagar.

    Maharashtra

    Maharashtra is known for its Bollywood industry, beaches, and temples. The capital of Maharashtra is Mumbai.

    Goa

    Goa is known for its beaches, nightlife, and cuisine. The capital of Goa is Panaji.

    Dadra and Nagar Haveli

    Dadra and Nagar Haveli is known for its natural beauty and wildlife. The capital of Dadra and Nagar Haveli is Silvassa.

    Daman and Diu

    Daman and Diu is known for its beaches and colonial architecture. The capital of Daman and Diu is Daman.

    The Southern States

    The southern region of India has five states. Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana.

    Karnataka

    Karnataka is known for its architecture, culture, and cuisine. The capital of Karnataka is Bangalore.

    Tamil Nadu

    Tamil Nadu is known for its temples, beaches, and cuisine. The capital of Tamil Nadu is Chennai.

    Kerala

    Kerala is known for its backwaters, beaches, and cuisine. The capital of Kerala is Thiruvananthapuram.

    Andhra Pradesh

    Andhra Pradesh is known for its historical monuments, beaches, and cuisine. The capital of Andhra Pradesh is Amaravati.

    Telangana

    Telangana is known for its technology, culture, and cuisine. The capital of Telangana is Hyderabad.

    Conclusion

    Knowing the India map with states and their capitals is essential to understand the geography and culture of the country. We have covered all 28 states and 8 union territories of India in this article. Each state has its own unique culture, language, and traditions. We hope this article has helped you in understanding the map of India with states and their capitals in 2023.

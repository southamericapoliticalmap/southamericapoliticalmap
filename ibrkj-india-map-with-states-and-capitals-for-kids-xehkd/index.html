    India Map With States And Capitals For Kids

    Posted on January 7, 2023 by admin

    Introduction

    India is a country located in South Asia, known for its rich culture, traditions, and diversity. It is the seventh-largest country in the world and is home to 28 states and 8 union territories. For kids, learning about India’s geography can be fun and exciting. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on India’s map with states and capitals for kids.

    What is a Map?

    Before we dive into the details of India’s map, let us understand what a map is. A map is a visual representation of an area, showing its physical features, roads, and other details. Maps are used to find directions, study geography, and understand the world around us.

    India’s Map with States and Capitals

    India’s map is divided into 28 states and 8 union territories. Each state has its own capital city. Here is a list of the states and their capital cities:

    Andhra Pradesh

    Capital: Amaravati

    Andhra Pradesh is a state located in the southeastern part of India. Its capital city, Amaravati, is known for its Buddhist heritage sites and is a popular tourist destination.

    Arunachal Pradesh

    Capital: Itanagar

    Arunachal Pradesh is a state located in the northeastern part of India. Its capital city, Itanagar, is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular destination for trekking and hiking enthusiasts.

    Nagaland is a state located in northeastern India. Its capital city, Kohima, is known for its scenic beauty, wildlife sanctuaries, and cultural festivals.

    Odisha

    Punjab

    Punjab is a state located in northern India. Its capital city, Chandigarh, is a union territory and is known for its cultural heritage and cuisine.

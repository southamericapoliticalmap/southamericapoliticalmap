    Airports In New York State Map

    Posted on March 27, 2023 by admin

    Introduction

    New York State is home to several airports, both large and small, that cater to millions of passengers every year. Whether you’re traveling for business or leisure, it’s important to know the location of the airports in New York State. In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide to the airports in New York State map.

    Major Airports in New York State Map

    New York State is home to three major airports: John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). These airports are located in the New York City metropolitan area and are among the busiest airports in the world.

    John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)

    JFK is located in Queens, New York, and is the largest airport in the New York City metropolitan area. It serves as a hub for several airlines, including Delta Air Lines and JetBlue Airways. JFK has six terminals and offers flights to destinations around the world.

    LaGuardia Airport (LGA)

    LGA is located in Queens, New York, and is the closest airport to Manhattan. It serves as a hub for several airlines, including American Airlines and Delta Air Lines. LGA has four terminals and offers flights to destinations across the United States.

    Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

    EWR is located in Newark, New Jersey, and is the third-largest airport in the New York City metropolitan area. It serves as a hub for several airlines, including United Airlines and FedEx Express. EWR has three terminals and offers flights to destinations around the world.

    Regional Airports in New York State Map

    New York State is also home to several regional airports that offer flights to nearby destinations.

    Albany International Airport (ALB)

    ALB is located in Albany, New York, and is the major airport serving New York’s Capital Region. It offers flights to several destinations across the United States.

    Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF)

    BUF is located in Buffalo, New York, and offers flights to several destinations across the United States and Canada.

    Rochester International Airport (ROC)

    ROC is located in Rochester, New York, and offers flights to several destinations across the United States.

    Syracuse Hancock International Airport (SYR)

    SYR is located in Syracuse, New York, and offers flights to several destinations across the United States and Canada.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, New York State is home to several airports that offer flights to destinations around the world. Whether you’re traveling for business or leisure, it’s important to know the location of the airports in New York State map. We hope that this comprehensive guide has been helpful in planning your next trip to New York State.

    Introduction

    Are you planning to visit Ukraine in 2023? If yes, then you must have a map of Ukraine to help you navigate your way around the country. One of the cities that you must visit is Melitopol, a beautiful city located in the southern part of Ukraine. In this article, we will provide you with a map of Ukraine showing Melitopol and some tips on how to get there, where to stay, and things to do in the city.

    Where is Melitopol?

    Melitopol is located in the Zaporizhia Oblast in the southern part of Ukraine. It is about 160 kilometers southeast of the city of Zaporizhia and about 730 kilometers southeast of the capital city of Kiev. Melitopol is situated on the Molochna River and is surrounded by beautiful hills and forests.

    How to Get to Melitopol?

    The easiest way to get to Melitopol is by taking a flight to the nearest airport, which is the Zaporizhia International Airport. From there, you can take a taxi or a bus to Melitopol. You can also take a train from Kiev to Melitopol, which takes about 12 hours.

    Where to Stay in Melitopol?

    There are several hotels and guesthouses in Melitopol that cater to tourists. Some of the most popular hotels include the Hotel Europa, Hotel Melitopol, and Hotel De La Paix. If you are looking for a more budget-friendly option, you can stay at one of the many hostels in the city.

    Things to Do in Melitopol

    Melitopol has a lot to offer tourists, especially those who love nature and outdoor activities. One of the top attractions in the city is the Molochna River, where you can go fishing, kayaking, and swimming. You can also visit the Melitopol Park, which is a beautiful park located in the heart of the city. If you are interested in history and culture, you can visit the Melitopol Museum of Local Lore, which showcases the history and traditions of the city and its people. You can also visit the Holy Transfiguration Cathedral, which is a beautiful Orthodox church located in the city center.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, Melitopol is a beautiful city that you must visit when you are in Ukraine in 2023. With the help of the map of Ukraine showing Melitopol and the tips provided in this article, you can easily navigate your way around the city and enjoy all the sights and sounds that it has to offer. So, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable trip to Ukraine!

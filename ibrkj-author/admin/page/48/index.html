Visitors can explore ancient monasteries and churches, hike in the mountains, or relax by the shores of Lake Sevan.     Politics

    Armenia is a parliamentary democracy, with a president as the head of state and a prime minister as the head of government. The country has a multi-party system, with a number of political parties represented in the National Assembly.     Latest Map of Texas Tornado Activity

    How to Stay Safe During a Tornado

    If you live in an area prone to tornadoes, it is important to have a plan in place for when severe weather strikes. Here are some tips for staying safe during a tornado:

    1. Find Shelter

    The safest place to be during a tornado is in a basement or storm shelter. If you do not have access to either, seek shelter in an interior room on the lowest level of your home or building, such as a closet or bathroom.

    2. Stay Informed

    Keep a radio or TV tuned to a local station for the latest weather updates. You can also sign up for text or email alerts from your local emergency management agency.

    3. Prepare an Emergency Kit

    Make sure you have an emergency kit on hand that includes items such as water, non-perishable food, a first-aid kit, and a flashlight.

    4. Stay Away from Windows

    During a tornado, windows can break and cause injury. Stay away from windows and seek shelter in an interior room on the lowest level of your home or building.

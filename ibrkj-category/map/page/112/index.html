The Kaziranga National Park in Assam is home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros and is a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts.     Getting Around the North East

    While the North East is a relatively small region, it can be challenging to navigate due to its rugged terrain and poor infrastructure. However, there are several options for getting around, including buses, taxis, and shared jeeps. For those who prefer a more leisurely pace, there are also several scenic train routes that offer breathtaking views of the landscape.

    Food and Drink in the North East

    The North East is known for its spicy cuisine, which features a variety of meats, fish, and vegetables. Popular dishes include momos (dumplings), thukpa (noodle soup), and bamboo shoot pickle. The region is also home to a variety of locally brewed rice beer, which is a must-try for any visitor.

    Shopping in the North East

    The North East is a treasure trove of handicrafts and textiles, with each state specializing in its own unique products. From intricately woven shawls to handmade pottery, there is something for everyone in the region’s bustling markets and bazaars.

    The Future of the North East

    Indiana State Fair Parking Map

    Posted on November 28, 2022 by admin

    Introduction

    The Indiana State Fair is one of the most highly anticipated events in the state. Every year, thousands of people flock to the Indiana State Fairgrounds to experience the festivities. However, finding parking can be a hassle. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide to the Indiana State Fair parking map for 2023.

    Parking Lots

    There are several parking lots available at the Indiana State Fairgrounds. The main parking lot is located on the west side of the fairgrounds, and it can accommodate up to 12,000 vehicles. There are also several smaller parking lots located throughout the fairgrounds, including designated areas for disabled parking.

    Parking Fees

    Parking fees vary depending on the day and time of arrival. On weekdays, parking fees are $10 per vehicle. On weekends and holidays, parking fees are $15 per vehicle. However, there are discounts available for those who purchase advance parking passes online.

    Alternative Parking Options

    If you’re looking to save some money on parking, there are several alternative parking options available. The Indiana State Fair offers a free shuttle service from several off-site parking locations, including the Glendale Mall and the Lafayette Square Mall.

    Bike Parking

    If you’re looking for a more eco-friendly way to get to the fair, there is a designated bike parking area located near the main entrance. Bike parking is free, and there are several bike racks available for use.

    RV Parking

    For those who are traveling in RVs, there are several RV parking options available. The Indiana State Fairgrounds offers both electric and non-electric RV parking, and there are several dump stations located throughout the fairgrounds.

    Tips for Finding Parking

    To make your parking experience at the Indiana State Fair as smooth as possible, here are a few tips to keep in mind: – Arrive early to secure a good parking spot – Consider purchasing an advance parking pass online to save money – Utilize the free shuttle service if possible – Follow the directions of the parking attendants to avoid congestion

    Conclusion

    The Indiana State Fair is a must-visit event for anyone living in or visiting the state. However, finding parking can be a challenge. By following the tips and information provided in this article, you can make your parking experience at the Indiana State Fair as stress-free as possible.

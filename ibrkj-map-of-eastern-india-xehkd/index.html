    Map Of Eastern India

    Posted on October 26, 2022 by admin

    Introduction

    India is a vast country with diverse cultures, languages, and traditions. Eastern India is one of the six regions of India and is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and cultural heritage. The map of Eastern India covers states like West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, and Bihar. It is essential to understand the geography of these states to plan a trip and explore the region thoroughly.

    West Bengal

    West Bengal is a state located in the eastern region of India. It is known for its cultural diversity, delicious cuisines, and historical significance. The capital city of West Bengal is Kolkata, which was formerly known as Calcutta. The state is bounded by the Himalayas in the north and the Bay of Bengal in the south. Some of the popular tourist destinations in West Bengal are Darjeeling, Sundarbans, and Victoria Memorial.

    Darjeeling

    Darjeeling is a hill station located in the northern part of West Bengal. It is known for its beautiful tea gardens, scenic views, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. The toy train ride in Darjeeling is a must-do activity for tourists.

    Sundarbans

    Sundarbans is a mangrove forest located in the southern part of West Bengal. It is the largest delta in the world and home to the Royal Bengal Tiger. The Sundarbans National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist destination.

    Odisha

    Odisha is a state located on the eastern coast of India. It is known for its ancient temples, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. The capital city of Odisha is Bhubaneswar, which is also known as the Temple City of India. Some of the popular tourist destinations in Odisha are Puri, Konark, and Chilika Lake.

    Puri

    Puri is a coastal town located in the eastern part of Odisha. It is famous for the Jagannath Temple, which is one of the four sacred places in Hinduism. The Puri Beach is also a popular tourist destination.

    Konark

    Konark is a small town located in the eastern part of Odisha. It is known for the Sun Temple, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The temple is designed in the shape of a chariot and is dedicated to the Hindu Sun God, Surya.

    Jharkhand

    Jharkhand is a state located in the eastern region of India. It is known for its beautiful waterfalls, dense forests, and wildlife sanctuaries. The capital city of Jharkhand is Ranchi, which is also known as the City of Waterfalls. Some of the popular tourist destinations in Jharkhand are Netarhat, Betla National Park, and Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary.

    Netarhat

    Netarhat is a hill station located in the eastern part of Jharkhand. It is known for its beautiful sunsets, natural beauty, and scenic views. The Netarhat Dam and the Upper Ghaghri Waterfalls are popular tourist attractions in Netarhat.

    Betla National Park

    Betla National Park is a wildlife sanctuary located in the western part of Jharkhand. It is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including tigers, elephants, and deer. The park is a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

    Bihar

    Bihar is a state located in the eastern region of India. It is known for its rich history, ancient ruins, and cultural heritage. The capital city of Bihar is Patna, which is also known as the City of Ancient Ruins. Some of the popular tourist destinations in Bihar are Bodhgaya, Nalanda, and Vaishali.

    Bodhgaya

    Bodhgaya is a small town located in the eastern part of Bihar. It is known for the Mahabodhi Temple, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The temple is a sacred place for Buddhists as it is believed to be the place where Buddha attained enlightenment.

    Nalanda

    Nalanda is a small town located in the eastern part of Bihar. It is known for the ancient Nalanda University, which was one of the oldest universities in the world. The university is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist destination.

    Conclusion

    The map of Eastern India covers states that are rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Exploring these states can be an enriching experience for tourists. This guide provides an overview of some of the popular tourist destinations in Eastern India. However, there is much more to explore in this region, and visitors should plan their trip accordingly.

    Discovering San Zaccaria Dock

    San Zaccaria Dock is located in Venice, Italy, and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the city. The dock is situated on the Riva degli Schiavoni, which is a waterfront promenade that runs along the edge of the San Marco district. San Zaccaria Dock is a popular starting point for many tours of Venice, including gondola rides and boat tours.

    Getting to San Zaccaria Dock

    To get to San Zaccaria Dock, you can take a waterbus, water taxi, or walk. If you’re taking a waterbus, you can take Line 1 or Line 2, which both stop at San Zaccaria. If you’re taking a water taxi, you can ask the driver to drop you off at San Zaccaria. If you’re walking, you can enter the San Marco district and follow the signs to San Zaccaria.

    Map Of Italy With Major Airports

    Posted on August 3, 2022 by admin

    Introduction

    Italy is a beautiful country with a rich history and culture. It is also a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors every year. If you’re planning a trip to Italy, one of the first things you’ll need to do is figure out how to get there. Fortunately, Italy has a well-developed transportation system, including several major airports that serve both domestic and international flights.

    The Map of Italy

    Italy is located in southern Europe and is bordered by France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia. The country’s shape is often compared to a boot, with the Italian peninsula extending into the Mediterranean Sea. The capital city of Italy is Rome, but there are several other major cities, including Milan, Naples, and Florence.

    Major Airports in Italy

    There are several major airports in Italy that serve both domestic and international flights. Some of the busiest airports include:

    Leonardo da Vinci International Airport – Rome

    This airport is located about 35 kilometers southwest of Rome and is the busiest airport in Italy. It serves as the main hub for Alitalia, Italy’s national airline, as well as several other airlines. The airport has four terminals and handles millions of passengers each year.

    Malpensa Airport – Milan

    Malpensa Airport is located about 50 kilometers northwest of Milan and is the second-busiest airport in Italy. It serves as a hub for several airlines, including EasyJet and Ryanair, and has two terminals. The airport offers flights to destinations all over the world.

    Naples International Airport – Naples

    Naples International Airport is located about 6 kilometers northeast of Naples and is the third-busiest airport in Italy. It serves as a hub for several airlines, including EasyJet and Ryanair, and has two terminals. The airport offers flights to destinations all over Europe and beyond.

    Conclusion

    If you’re planning a trip to Italy, it’s important to know which airports are the major ones. This will help you plan your travel itinerary and make sure you get to your destination smoothly. Whether you’re flying into Rome, Milan, Naples, or another Italian city, you’ll find that Italy’s airports are well-equipped with modern facilities and offer a range of services and amenities to make your travel experience as comfortable as possible.
